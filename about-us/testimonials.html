Dubai Drums visit to Rashid centre week of 17th June 2007

Dear Julie-Ann,

A group from Dubai Drums came to Rashid Paediatric Therapy Centre in June of 2007, bringing drums for every child in the Centre. The communication that followed between the drummer-in-charge for the session and the children was on a level, and of a standard that was very impressive.

Communication is one of our prime aims, as many pupils are non-verbal, and so it was of tremendous benefit to the pupils here to use the skills aleady developed from our own interactive music therapy communication sessions, in a different context.

The children responded in a positive way to vocal, musical and rhymthic directions, and were involved throughout the session. Some of the skills we teach here in terms of listening and following instructions were incorporated. Turn taking, awareness of fast and slow, loud, quiet, start and stop, to name but a few more skills were also used.

Not only were the pupils enthralled, but the staff made very positive comments about pupil focus of attention and interaction between drummers and pupils. I have invited Dubai Drums to the Centre in the next academic year, and we all await the next session eagerly.

Janette S. Graham
Head Teacher
Rashid Paediatric Therapy Centre - Dubai
