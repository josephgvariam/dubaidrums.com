2014 is set to start off on the right beat as we bring in the new year together in the best way we know how: drumming!

Every year, Dubai Drums sets the new year off to the right beat, by drumming in lots of positive spirit and happiness in a fun-filled, diverse and entertaining experience where everyone present becomes a part of the evening's entertainment.

Come and share rhythmic spirit together and enjoy a night full of drumming, singing, dancing and general merriment with great people, good food, super music and lots of new year spirit!

The camp will be decorated new year style and the bar stocked at reasonable prices. There will be a bonfire but bring warm clothes as well.

This year we will be hosting the New year party at our favourite camp, the Gulf Ventures Desert Camp in Al Awir off the Hatta Road.

Price includes community drumming throughout the night, dinner, camel rides, sand boarding, shisha, belly dancer, fire dance show, african drum group, DJ Capt Guy, kids activities and much more.
